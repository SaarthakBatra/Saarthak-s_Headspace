## Alternative income from bamboo
1. Biogas production from bamboo, output is electricity and coal
2. Coal can be resold to electricity plants
3. Coal can be converted to biochar and used as fertilizer or activated charcoal
4. Use as biochar gives carbon credits = $200 per credit
5. 2G Ethanol can be created from bamboo, since its not an agricultural food crop it does not compete with maize or sugar cane
6. Flexible harvests in bamboo any time of the year
7. IH2 (hydropyrolisis) process to produce petrol from bamboo
