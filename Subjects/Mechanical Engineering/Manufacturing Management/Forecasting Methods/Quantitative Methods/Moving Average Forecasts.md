Forecast is the average of previous months
- Best for short planning with no major trends, seasonal patterns or business patterns
- As value of "$n$" increases, forecast become smoother but less sensitive to recent changes
$$\text{Moving Average}( MN(n) ) = \frac{\sum d_i}{n}$$
- where, 
	- $n$ = number of periods
