We know that, length of weld required to withstand load P follows relation,
$$P = 0.707\ h\ l\ \tau$$
Hence the sum of the two asymmetrical welds must be equal to this length, i.e
$$l_1+l_2=l$$
and, by conservation of momentum and angular momentum we have
$$
\begin{align}
P = &P_1+P_2,\\
P_iy_i &= P_2y_2,\\
l_1y_1&=l_2y_2
\end{align}
$$
