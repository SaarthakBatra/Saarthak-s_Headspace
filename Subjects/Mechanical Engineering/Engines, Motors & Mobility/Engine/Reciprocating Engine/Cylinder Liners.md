- The cylinder liner is a sleeve in which the piston of an engine reciprocates.
- The life of a cylinder between its re-bores depends on two main factors:
	1. Abrasion
	2. Corrosion
- For cylinder liners nickel-chromium iron has been popularly used.
- The nickel-chromium iron used contains
	1. carbon 3.5%
	2. manganese 0.6%
	3. phosphorous 1.5%
	4. sulphur 0.05%
	5. silicon 2%
	6. nickel 2%
	7. chromium 0.7%
- The cylinder liners or sleeves are of two types:
	1. Dry liners
	2. Wet liners
