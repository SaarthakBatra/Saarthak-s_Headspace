The engine gets it's motive force from the combustion or the burning of mixture of fuel and oxygen inside an engine. The part of engine where combustion occurs is known as Piston-cylinder or  combustion chamber.
## Types
1. Spark ignition engine
2. Compression ignition engine

## Classification of reciprocating IC engines
1. Cycle of Opertaion
	1. Otto Cycle Engine (SI Engine / Gasoline Engine)
	2. Diesel Cycle Engine (CI Engine / Diesel Engine)

2. Number of Strokes per Cycle
	1. 2-Stroke Engine
	2. 4-Stroke Engine

3. Types of Fuel
	1. Liquid Fuels (Gasoline, Diesel, Alcohol, Kerosene)
	2. Gaseous Fuels (CNG, LPG, Blast Furnace Gas, Biogas)

4. Method of Ignition
	1. Spark Ignition Engine (SI Engine)
	2. Compression Ignition Engine (CI Engine)

5. Operating Speed
	1. Low Speed Engine
	2. Medium Speed Engine
	3. High Speed Engine

6. Method of Cooling
	1. Air Cooled Engine
	2. Water Cooled Engine

7. Field of Application
	1. Stationary Engine
	2. Automotive Engine
	3. Marine Engine
	4. Aero Engine
	5. Locomotive Engine
